Persona

Bio

Name: Caro Mayer

Profession: Geriatric Nurses

Needs

Caro wants to be able to check her daily and weekly schedule on a mobile phone and update her task like when she is driving to a patient, with a patient, and finished caring for a patient. And she can add extra activities that were not listed on a particular patient which she performed.

Goals

  1. Log in to the app
  2. Click check schedule
    • Click Today Schedule to check the specific day schedule
    • Click This Week to check the specific week schedule
  3. click Start Tour to begin the day schedule.
  4. Click Departure when going to the next patient on the queue
  5. Click Arrived when she is with patient
  6. Click Treatment when treating the patient
  7. Click Treatment Completed when through with a patient.
  8. And click Departure to indicate when leaving the current patient and going to the next patient on the queue
