Critique

Outpatient App

Advantages

  1. Create “Tribes” for each resident; including family, friends, care providers, and others on a need-to-know basis.
  2. Share updates with Tribe members – all at once, individually or a select group
  3. Improve communications between care staff and resident tribes
  4. Track and monitor individul patient treatment/care progress

Disadvantages

  1. A nurse can not monitor his/her daily progress
  2. No functionality to for the supervisor to monitor the activites a nurse in real-time
  3. A nurse can not transfer his or her patient to another nurse
  4. No systematic way of transiting from one pattient to another in the app.
  5. No functionality for the nurse to create a schedule except for the patient
